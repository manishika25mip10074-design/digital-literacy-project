Prevention Checklist:



* Never disclose UPI PIN and OTP to anyone
* Don’t allow unknown payment requests
* Don’t click on suspicious URLs
* Verify the caller if someone claims to be a bank officer
* Don’t scan suspicious QR codes
* Use official apps to make transactions
* Verify bank statements
* Activate app-based security features like PIN and fingerprint
* Report fraud to cybercrime.gov.in
* Contact helpline number 1930 in case of financial fraud
